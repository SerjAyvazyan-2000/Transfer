Berlin Airport Pickup Service and Private Transfers

You can get to other cities in Germany from this very airport, e.g., Munich, Cologne, Frankfurt. Have a route from the Berlin BER Airport? This airport is considered to be the second international airport in the city, following BER. In particular, it is used for low-cost flights via Ryanair and EasyJet and can be easily reached by planes of flagship carriers. It is located only 22.7 km from downtown, and the total time to get there takes 45 minutes provided that there are no traffic jams.
